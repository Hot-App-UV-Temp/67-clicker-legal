Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the "67 Clicker" app handles information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Local Data Only

67 Clicker is designed with privacy in mind. The app collects and stores the following data only on your device:

  • Total Click Count: Your cumulative clicking progress
  • Timer Session Data: Click counts during timer sessions
  • App Settings: Timer duration preferences

No Personal Information

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

How We Use Information

The data collected is used solely to:

  • Maintain your clicking progress between app sessions
  • Provide timer functionality
  • Enhance your gaming experience

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage systems (UserDefaults). No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security measures. We recommend keeping your device updated and using appropriate security measures.

Third-Party Services

67 Clicker does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from users of any age.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • Delete the app to remove all associated data
  • Reset your progress through the app settings
  • Control data sharing through your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via hot.app.contact@gmail.com.

Compliance

This privacy policy complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including GDPR and CCPA principles, by minimizing data collection and ensuring user control.
